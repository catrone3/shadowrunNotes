== Assets ==

Orbital facilities include [https://shadowrun.fandom.com/wiki/Apollo Apollo], Icarus, [https://shadowrun.fandom.com/wiki/Daedalus Daedalus], Artemis, and [https://shadowrun.fandom.com/wiki/Helios Helios].
